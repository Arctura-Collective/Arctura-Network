# Arctura Collective

> Building regenerative futures at the intersection of technology, design, and community.

## About

**Arctura Collective** is a decentralized network of engineers, designers, researchers, artists, and organizers. We prototype open tools, publish field notes, and run small pilots that help communities move toward low-impact, high-resilience systems.

This repository is the public handbook: mission, active projects, structure, and how to contribute.

## Mission

- **Prototype in public**: Share early, document thoroughly, learn faster
- **Regenerate, don't extract**: Design for ecological and social surplus
- **Keep it open**: Default to open-source code, open hardware, and open knowledge

## What We Do

- **Research & Prototyping**: Climate tech, distributed energy, materials, and low-emission chemical processes
- **Open Source**: Firmware, hardware references, process models, datasets, and design systems
- **Collective Practice**: Peer learning circles, residencies, and open calls
- **Field Work**: Pilots and deployments with local partners
